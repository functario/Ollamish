Here’s a classic **lemon cake** recipe that's moist, fluffy, and full of bright lemon flavor:

---

### 🍋 **Lemon Cake Recipe**

#### **Ingredients**

**For the Cake:**
- 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter, softened  
- 1 ½ cups (300g) granulated sugar  
- 4 large eggs  
- 2 tablespoons lemon zest (from 2 lemons)  
- ¼ cup (60ml) fresh lemon juice  
- 2 ½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour  
- 2 ½ teaspoons baking powder  
- ½ teaspoon salt  
- 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk (or milk + 1 tbsp vinegar as substitute)

**For the Lemon Glaze:**
- 1 cup (120g) powdered sugar  
- 2–3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice  

---

#### **Instructions**

1. **Preheat oven** to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5 inch loaf pan or two 8-inch round pans.

2. **Cream the butter and sugar** together in a large bowl until light and fluffy.

3. Add eggs **one at a time**, beating well after each. Mix in lemon zest and lemon juice.

4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.

5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with buttermilk. Begin and end with flour mixture. Mix just until combined.

6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan(s) and smooth the top.

7. **Bake** for 45–55 minutes (loaf) or 25–30 minutes (round pans), until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.

8. Let cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.

9. **Mix the glaze** ingredients until smooth. Drizzle over warm or cooled cake.
